======
Unary operation on variable with splat expression
======

foo = !var.bar

------

(config_file
  (body
    (attribute
      (identifier)
      (unary_operation
        (get_attr
          (identifier)
          (identifier))))))

=====
Attribute as conditional expression
=====

foo = local.bar == local.baz

-----

(config_file
  (body
    (attribute
      (identifier)
      (binary_operation
        (get_attr
          (identifier)
          (identifier))
        (get_attr
          (identifier)
          (identifier))))))
